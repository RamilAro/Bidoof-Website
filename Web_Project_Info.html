Fun Facts

  • Bidoof is the Plump Mouse Pokemon

  • Bidoof is from the 4th Generation of Pokemon Games
    They are short, rodent-like Pokemon that have brown fur,
    and cream-colored markings on its underside and face.
    Thier diet consists of rocks and wood.




  • Bidoofs live in their nests near rivers.

  • As you can see in the picture, they are making a dam.
    This is because Bidoofs are based of the real life animal,
    Beavers!




  • Bidoofs' teeth are constantly growing.

  • In the picture, some Bidoofs are gnawing
    on rocks and wood,this is how they keep their teeth short.
    When they do not do this, their teeth
    would become a hindrance in their daily lives.




  • The round tufts of hair in their tails indicate their gender.

  • In the picture, we can see 5 bidoofs,
    there are 3 with 3 round tufts, this means they are females.
    We can clearly see 1 with 5 round tufts
    and the other, although cut off in the image, has 5
    which means they are males.
